How can I participate?

1. Take a photo that represents who you are and what you love about winter.

2. Join our discord server! Click here!

3. Make sure to become a club member. Apply.

4. Post it on Instagram with the hashtag #uOttawaPhotoClub and tag @uophotography_club.
You can also submit your photo on our Discord server.

5. Please continue to share your love for photography, even if you've made your submission!
Who knows? Maybe one of your pieces will end up in our gallery!

- - - - - - -

Even if you aren't a member, you can still join our Discord server to talk with like-minded
people who love photography and share your photos with us. Come and say hi!
