Terms & Conditions

Please read the constitution of the club that was put together by the council.
This bilingual document will give you a rather decent idea on how the club will
run and organize events.

Find it here.

The council reserves the right to remove any member who willingly participates
in hateful and destructive activities. Such as, but not limited to:
harassment, racism, sexism, hate speech, and all other targeted or general hate attacks.


If you accept your admission into the club, you agree to the following:

  • You will be respectful all other members.
  • If you choose to participate in an event, you will follow the event's rules.
  • You allow the council to post publically your submissions on the club's social pages, website and vote forms.


All in all, we want a welcoming and fun club. So let's keep it that way.

Privacy

By filling the application form, you will provide with the club some personal information.
You agree that the club may be able to use the information that you decide
to submit for your consideration into the club.

The club council will NEVER use the information
received for any purpose other than for application consideration.

Questions?

You can contact us at uophotoclub@gmail.com or on any of our socials listed on the home page.
